Can Bankruptcy Stop an Eviction?

A free guide for Missouri residents considering bankruptcy. Filed in E.D. Mo..

What You Need to Know

The automatic stay under Section 362 temporarily halts most eviction proceedings. However, Section 362(b)(22) limits protection if your landlord already has a judgment for possession. In Chapter 13, you may be able to cure rent arrears through your plan. Timing matters - file before the judgment if possible.

Filed in E.D. Mo.. Courthouse: 111 S 10th St, St. Louis MO.
