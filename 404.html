Quick tip: If you live in the Illinois side of the St. Louis metro (Belleville, Edwardsville, etc.), you file in the S.D. Ill. and use Illinois exemptions -- which include an unlimited wildcard for retirement accounts and a $15,000 homestead exemption. The district you file in depends on where you live, not where you work.
